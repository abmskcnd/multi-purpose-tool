## üìà Market Trends

### Tailwinds (Opportunities)

1. **Privacy Awakening**
   - GDPR/CCPA awareness increasing
   - Data breach news making users cautious
   - **Opportunity:** Privacy-first positioning

2. **Mobile-First Usage**
   - 60%+ traffic from mobile
   - Users expect seamless experience
   - **Opportunity:** Mobile-optimized wins

3. **Remote Work Growth**
   - WFH increases digital tool need
   - File conversion more common
   - **Opportunity:** Target remote workers

### Headwinds (Threats)

1. **Ad-Blocker Adoption**
   - 30-40% users block ads
   - **Mitigation:** Premium tier, affiliate

2. **Big Tech Competition**
   - Google Squoosh (free, excellent)
   - Adobe Express (freemium)
   - **Mitigation:** Multi-tool platform vs single-purpose
