## ðŸŽª Product Strategy Framework

### Strategic Bets

#### Bet #1: Privacy-First Wins Long-Term
**Hypothesis:** Users increasingly care about privacy (GDPR, data breaches)  
**Validation:** Survey users vá» willingness to pay for privacy  
**Risk:** Privacy might not be primary driver (convenience wins)  
**Mitigation:** Also compete on speed & ease of use

#### Bet #2: SEO-Led Growth is Scalable
**Hypothesis:** Programmatic SEO can drive 80%+ organic traffic  
**Validation:** Competitor analysis shows 70-90% organic  
**Risk:** Google algorithm changes hurt rankings  
**Mitigation:** Diversify channels (social, referral, direct)

#### Bet #3: Free + Ads is Sustainable
**Hypothesis:** Ad revenue at scale (500K MAU) = $5K MRR  
**Validation:** Industry benchmarks: $1-2 RPM for tools  
**Risk:** Ad blockers, low CPM in certain geos  
**Mitigation:** Introduce affiliate + premium tiers
